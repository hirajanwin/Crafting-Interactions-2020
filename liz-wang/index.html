Keys for Kids

An iPad mobile application allowing piano students to better learn and communicate with their piano teachers. This platform is a tool to help children practice their piano assignments and share their progress with their parents and teachers.

Problem statement

How might we help children interested in learning piano improve their technical skills?

Created based on our own experience learning piano and assumptions about current piano learning experience.

From creating the user journey, we decided to focus our scope on the practicing face because it seems that how well kids practice dictate how they feel when they meet with their teacher and feel about their piano skills.
