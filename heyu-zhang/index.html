Overview

Keys for kids is a project that my teammate and I worked on for Crafting Interactions at SVA.

It is a platform that provides an easy way for kids to practice piano. It is a learning tool that allows kids to look at their mistakes and performance, and share the recordings with teachers to get feedback in real time.

User Journey

Based on our research in kids who practices piano and take weekly lessons with their teachers, we mapped out the user journey and identified the key moments and pain points throughout their experience of taking classes, getting feedback, and practicing the piano at home.

User Testing & Feedback

We created the first version of high fidelity design of Keys for Kids, and tested with 2 users to gather qualitative feedback on the flow and detailed functionalities. The testing provided us with great insights on how we could improve certain features within the app.
