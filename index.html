Crafting Interactions is a course taught at the SVA Interaction Design MFA program. This course examines how to integrate prototyping, as well as research and testing techniques, into every stage of the interaction design process. Because of COVID-19, the latter half of the semester was conducted online. Please check out the amazing projects students have crafted despite the hardship they faced.
