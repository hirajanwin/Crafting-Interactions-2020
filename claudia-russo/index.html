What does the life of the elderly look like?

CONTEXT

I remember those times when my great-aunt got into fights with my uncles and dad because they didn't visit that often or take her grocery shopping. They all felt it was a cumbersome task. I was quite small to understand what these arguments where about; I probably also felt frustrated that I couldn't hang out longer with my friends because we had to stop by her place.

Now as an adult, I've realized how difficult it was for her to get social interactions. She was a widow who couldn't drive, and was loosing her vision and hearing. What else was left for her to do?

And this happens everywhere, take the United States as an example. Research shows that 11 million people aged 65 and older are living alone. Around 25% of those who reported feeling lonely mentioned they had trouble carrying out daily activities.

According to AARP reports, more and more older adults don't have children, which translates into fewer family members to provide company and care. But, at this point social contacts tend to decrease as we age for other reasons too, such as:

  • Death of friends and family members
  • Retirement
  • Lack of mobility

Around 70% of seniors are connected to the internet.

BUT...THEY ARE KEEPING UP

Around 70% of seniors are connected to the internet. Their devices are a part of their daily lives to stay informed, connect to friends and family, shop, and make reservations for travel and transportation.

Some of the apps they use are Medisafe, Google Maps with parking spot reminders, HomeAway and Lumosity.

According to Pew Research’s Tech Adoption Among Older Adults, 58% of adults ages 65 and older say technology has had a mostly positive impact on society, while roughly three-quarters of internet-using seniors say they go online on a daily basis.

My Essentials

Create and manage the opportunities other heroes see.

Users will add a short description of what they need. Then, choose what they are interested in. If they want others to know their background, they can add their superpowers.

Lastly, they will be able to preview and post this opportunity.

Opportunities

Offer to help someone in need or build valuable connections with people that can help you in different areas of your life.
‍
To find opportunities, users will browse the opportunities sections. Then, send a message request with details of how they can help.

Receive Help Offers

Accept requests to find out more details. Or, just meet someone new!

If a request is accepted, both parties will be able to communicate and join their Heroes list automatically.
